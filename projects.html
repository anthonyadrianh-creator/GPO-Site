Historic Validation

Guyana: Home to Some of the World's Largest Gold Deposits

Gold Port's wholly owned Groete Gold Project has an extensive exploration history. Drilled by the United Nations in the 1960s and Coeur d'Alene Mines Ltd. in the 1990s, the project has emerged as one of Guyana's largest gold copper zones, and the only deposit with deep water access.

Current Resource Estimate

1.57 million gold equivalent ounces within 74 million tonnes at a grade of 0.66 g/t AuEq using a cut-off grade of 0.25 g/t, based on US$1,275/oz gold and US$3.00/lb copper prices.

Details from National Instrument 43-101 report titled Technical Report and Updated Mineral Resource Estimate on the Groete Gold Copper Deposit by P&E Mining Consultants Ltd., dated April 16, 2019.

Surface Evaluation Results

Comprehensive surface evaluation completed on eastern zone with new zones of interest identified:

  • 52 deep soil holes up to 8.75 meters in depth
  • Significant results: Up to 2.65 g/t gold over 8.75 meters (surface down)
  • 47 of 52 holes anomalous for gold (over 0.10 g/t)
  • 23 holes greater than 0.50 g/t gold average
